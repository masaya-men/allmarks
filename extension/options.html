AllMarks settings

Auto-save on SNS button

When you press one of these buttons, the page is automatically saved to AllMarks. Already-saved URLs are skipped silently.

Cursor pill position when no mouse data

Floating save button

A small mark sits on the edge of every page. Click to save the page to AllMarks. Long-press to drag and release — it snaps to the nearest left or right edge. Already-saved pages show a permanent green check.

Hide on specific sites

Add a domain (e.g. example.com) to hide the floating button on that site only. Other save shortcuts still work.

Keyboard shortcut

Default: Ctrl+Shift+B (Mac: Cmd+Shift+B)
